Sohom Chatterjee, Ph.D.

I am a PhD graduate from the Industrial and Systems Engineering Department in Texas A&M University, specialized in the field of Operations Research. I am currently working as a Research Engineer in the Risk Analytics and Optimization lab at GE Aerospace Research. I am interested in the applications of large-scale discrete optimization problems, specifically healthcare and supply chain.
