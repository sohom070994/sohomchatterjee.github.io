Hi. I'm Sohom Chatterjee.

I'm a Ph.D. candidate at the Industrial and Systems Engineering Department in Texas A&M University, specializing in the field of Operations Research. I am interested in the applications of large-scale discrete optimization problems, specifically healthcare and supply chain.
